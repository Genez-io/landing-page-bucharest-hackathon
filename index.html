About The Bucharest Hackathon

The Bucharest Hackathon is a dynamic weekend-long event where like minded programmers from Romania and the region gather to work on groundbreaking software projects and solve tomorrow's coding challenges using the latest, most cutting-edge technologies available.

This year we're going all in on bridging the gap between the digital realm and the real world via blockchain, AI and devtools. We bring the resources, the fuel, the mentors and support you need to learn new skills and build a unique product. Have fun with friends, and make new ones, while experimenting with new-age technologies!

And that's not all! You will also get the chance to meet and have a chat with our partners, who are leading the push for new and inspiring technologies.

Join us to build your first project based on Web3 and OpenAI! Your task is to imagine a future iteration of our tech world and write history one line of code at a time. Are you up to the challenge?

Whether you already know your way around blockchain, AI, devtools or you're just starting, grab a seat, the event is open to all tech enthusiasts!

The Bucharest Hackathon requires physical attendance and you will be able to participate in teams of 2 to 4 members. If you already have a team, you can enroll here or enroll on your own and we'll find worthy teammates for you. The registration is free, you have everything to gain and nothing to lose!
