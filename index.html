About The Bucharest Hackathon

The Bucharest Hackathon is a weekend-long event where hundreds of programmers from Romania and the region come together to work on cool software projects using the latest technologies. This year we are focusing on bridging the gap between the digital realm and the real world using blockchain and AI. Participants will have the occasion to interact with our partners, who play a big part in creating these technologies. Come and build your first app in this cool new space combining Web3 and OpenAI. You will be challenged to imagine the future and to write history in the first event of its kind.

Happy hacking!

BLOCKCHAIN

Index NFT transactions on the Ethereum blockchain
Check out a tutorial on how to write an indexing app on genezio repo.
TenderMint Blockchain Explorer
Details needed from the Bware team - example, provisioning a tender mint node, etc
Write an updated version of the Geth client
It allows data reorganisation into smaller ranges for read-only purposes. E.g. Access data between 0 and X blocks without having to load the entire Ethereum network. Allow for syncing with the following flags:
--block-to-sync-from
--partial-db-url
