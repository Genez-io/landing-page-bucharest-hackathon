Welcome to the third edition of The Bucharest Hackathon, a high-energy, weekend-long event where ambitious builders and innovators from Romania and across the region come together to shape the future of technology.

In 2026, we're venturing beyond code into the frontier of intelligent, decentralized innovation, where AI, blockchain, and next-generation dev tools converge to redefine how we build, connect, and create.

Over an intense 24 hours, you'll have access to expert mentors, cutting-edge APIs, and our partners' technologies to power your ideas. Whether you want to build an autonomous AI agent, a decentralized data marketplace, or a creative on-chain experience, this is your chance to prototype the future with a like-minded community.

The event is scheduled for April 13-14, 2024.

The Bucharest Hackathon will take place at Odeon Theatre - Studio Hall on 40-42 Calea Victoriei Bucharest 010062, Romania .
  • Our mobility partner, Blue, will take you directly to Odeon Theatre (if you are a participant, send us an email at info@bucharesthackathon to send you the discount code for the ride).
  • Bus 100 from the Airport to the Piata 21 December 1989 Bus Stop. Once there, walk for about 5 minutes until you reach Odeon Theatre
  • Train until North Railway Station (Gara de Nord). Then the yellow subway line (M1) to the “Victoriei” station, followed by the blue subway line (M2) to the “Universitate” station. Once there, walk for about 8 minutes until you reach Odeon Theatre
